What to include in your message

If you are reporting a problem, include the page URL, the device or browser you used, and a short description of what happened. This makes it easier to find broken links, display issues, or tool behavior that needs improvement.

If you are suggesting a new generator, include the niche, the kind of names people would search for, and any examples of styles that would be useful. The site is expanded around practical topics that visitors can use immediately.

Common reasons to get in touch

  • Report a broken link, typo, or page that does not load correctly.
  • Suggest a new name generator or article topic.
  • Ask a general question about how to use the tools.
  • Share feedback about confusing content, missing examples, or unclear wording.

Important note about names

The site provides brainstorming ideas only. It cannot confirm trademark availability, domain ownership, business registration status, or social handle rights. Before using any name for a company, product, channel, or public project, run your own checks with the relevant platforms and official databases.

Messages are reviewed as time allows. This is a free tool and content site, so responses may not be immediate, but useful reports and suggestions help improve the site for everyone.
