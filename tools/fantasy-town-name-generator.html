      Tips

      How to use this tool

      Town names should hint at geography, history, or what makes the place special. "Frosthollow" suggests a cold valley. "Brightwater" suggests a clean river.

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Make sure it sounds like a real place.
      • Check that it is not already used in a major franchise.
      • Consider the town's geography and history.
      • Test it on a hand-drawn map.

      FAQ

      Fantasy Town Name Generator FAQ

      How do you name a fantasy town?

      Combine a geographic feature or resource with a suffix like -haven, -vale, -crest, -moor, -gate, or -fen. This creates names that feel grounded and real.

      Should fantasy town names sound English?

      Not necessarily. Different cultures in your world should have different naming conventions. But for English-language readers, familiar patterns are easier to remember.
