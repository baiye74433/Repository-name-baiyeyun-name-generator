      FAQ

      Startup Name Generator FAQ

      What makes a good startup name?

      Short, easy to spell, available as a .com, and does not limit you to one product or market as you grow.

      Should a startup name describe what the company does?

      Not necessarily. Many successful startups have abstract names. What matters more is that it is memorable and easy to say.
