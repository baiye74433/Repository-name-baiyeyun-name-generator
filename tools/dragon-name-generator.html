      Tips

      How to use this dragon name name generator

      Dragon names should feel like they echo in a cave. The best ones are two to three syllables with at least one hard consonant sound.

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Say the name in a deep voice — does it feel powerful?
      • Check that it is not already a famous dragon from major franchises.
      • Make sure you can spell it consistently.
      • Consider how it sounds as a username or character name.

      FAQ

      Dragon Name Generator FAQ

      How do I name a dragon?

      Combine harsh consonants (x, z, v, th) with flowing vowels. Dragon names should feel powerful and ancient when spoken aloud.

      Should dragon names have meaning?

      In worldbuilding, yes — tying the name to an element, trait, or backstory makes it more memorable. For casual use, sound is enough.
