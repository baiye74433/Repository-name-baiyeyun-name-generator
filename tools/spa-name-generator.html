      FAQ

      Spa Name Generator FAQ

      What makes a good spa name?

      Spa names should evoke calm, luxury, and self-care. Words like "serenity," "bliss," "tranquil," and "harmony" are classic choices.

      Should I include "Spa" in the name?

      It helps with local SEO and clarity. But a strong brand name without it can work if the branding makes the purpose clear.
