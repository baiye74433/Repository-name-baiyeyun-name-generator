      Tips

      How to use this tool

      Wizard names should feel like they have been spoken in candlelit towers for centuries. They should sound like incantations themselves.

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Make it sound ancient and wise.
      • Check that it does not match a famous wizard.
      • Consider adding a title or epithet.
      • Test the full name: "[Name] the [Title]."

      FAQ

      Wizard Name Generator FAQ

      What makes a good wizard name?

      Wizard names should sound ancient and powerful. Long, flowing names with archaic elements (Aldric, Meridia, Tharion) work well.

      Should wizards have titles?

      Titles like "the Great," "the Wise," or "of the East Tower" add depth and make the character more memorable.
