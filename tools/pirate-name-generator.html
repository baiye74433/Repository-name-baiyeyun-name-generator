      Tips

      How to use this tool

      Pirate names should sound like they were earned through adventure. Think weathered, rough, and slightly dangerous.

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Make it sound rough and adventurous.
      • Check that it is not a famous existing pirate.
      • Consider the pirate's defining trait or weapon.
      • Test it as a ship name or crew name.

      FAQ

      Pirate Name Generator FAQ

      How do pirate names work?

      Pirate names often combine a trait, scar, or weapon with a common name. "One-Eye Morgan" or "Cutlass Jack" follow this pattern.

      Were real pirate names this dramatic?

      Some were. Blackbeard (Edward Teach) and Calico Jack (John Rackham) were real pirates with memorable nicknames.
