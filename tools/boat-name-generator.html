      Tips

      How to use this tool

      Boat names are a tradition. They should make other boaters smile at the dock. Puns, wordplay, and clever references are not just allowed — they are expected.

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Check your marina for duplicate names.
      • Make sure it looks good on the hull.
      • Keep it under 33 characters (USCG limit).
      • Consider how it sounds on a VHF radio call.

      FAQ

      Boat Name Generator FAQ

      What are popular boat name styles?

      Puns are extremely popular (Knot Working, Anchor Management). Other styles include nature names, nautical terms, and personal references.

      Are there rules for boat names?

      No universal rules, but keep it clean and readable. Some marinas have guidelines. The name must be registered with your country coast guard.
