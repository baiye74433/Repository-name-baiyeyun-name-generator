      FAQ

      Law Firm Name Generator FAQ

      How do law firms name themselves?

      Most use partner surnames (Smith & Jones), descriptive names (Metro Legal), or a combination. The trend is moving toward brand-style names.

      Can I use a creative name for my law firm?

      Depends on your jurisdiction. Some bar associations require partner names. Check your local rules before choosing a brand-style name.
