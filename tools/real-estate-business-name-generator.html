      FAQ

      Real Estate Business Name Generator FAQ

      How do I name my real estate business?

      Use words that evoke trust, stability, and growth. Location-based names work well for local agencies. Abstract names work better for national brands.

      Do I need "Realty" or "Real Estate" in the name?

      It helps with clarity and SEO, but is not legally required in most states. Check your local licensing requirements.
