      Tips

      How to use this tool

      Barbershop names should be easy to remember from a sign, map listing, or referral. Strong words like blade, comb, chair, cut, and crown create instant context.

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Make it easy to say over the phone.
      • Check local competitors on Google Maps.
      • Choose a name that fits your shop style.
      • Avoid names that sound like a hair salon if you focus on barbering.
