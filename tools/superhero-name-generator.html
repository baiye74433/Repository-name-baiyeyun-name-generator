      Tips

      How to use this tool

      Superhero names should sound like they belong on a comic book cover. Test them by imagining a dramatic announcement: "It's [name]!"

      Checklist

      A quick naming checklist

      • Say it dramatically — does it sound heroic?
      • Check Marvel and DC for existing characters.
      • Make sure it works as a domain and social handle.
      • Consider how it looks as a logo.

      FAQ

      Superhero Name Generator FAQ

      What makes a good superhero name?

      It should sound powerful, memorable, and hint at the hero's power or origin. Two-word names work best.

      Should the name reveal the power?

      Not always. "Spider-Man" does, but "Batman" is more mysterious. Both approaches work.
