      FAQ

      Food Truck Name Generator FAQ

      How do I name my food truck?

      Think about your signature dish, your cooking style, or a fun pun. Food truck names can be more playful than restaurant names.

      Should the name hint at the cuisine?

      Yes, ideally. When someone sees your truck from across a parking lot, they should have a rough idea of what you serve.
