Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what information may be collected when you use AI Name Generator.

AI Name Generator is a public content and tool website. You can browse the site, read articles, and use the name generators without creating an account or submitting personal information.

Information we may collect

The site may collect limited technical information through standard hosting, analytics, security, or performance tools. This may include pages visited, browser type, device type, referring pages, approximate region, and basic usage patterns.

This information helps understand which tools are useful, find technical problems, improve navigation, and keep the site working reliably.

Contact emails

If you contact the site by email, the information you choose to send may be used to respond to your message, investigate a reported issue, or improve site content. Do not send sensitive personal information, passwords, payment details, or private business data through email.

Cookies and third-party services

The site may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, security, or advertising. These services may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. Visitors can control cookies through their browser settings.

If advertising is displayed in the future, advertising partners may use cookies to serve, limit, and measure ads. This policy will continue to describe the general categories of data that may be processed by those services.

Generated names and tool inputs

The generators are designed for brainstorming. Keywords typed into a generator are used to create on-page name ideas. Do not enter confidential business plans, private names, passwords, account details, or other sensitive information into any public web tool.

Data retention

Server logs, analytics records, and email messages may be retained for a reasonable period for security, troubleshooting, communication, and site improvement. The site does not sell visitor contact details.

External links

Some pages may link to external websites. This privacy policy applies only to AI Name Generator. External websites have their own privacy practices and terms.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated as the site changes. The latest version will remain available on this page.
