WiFi Name Ideas: Funny, Clean, and Clever Names for Your Router

Your WiFi name is visible to everyone in range. Make it count. Here are funny, clever, and creative WiFi names for every taste.

Why WiFi Names Are Fun

Unlike most names on this site, WiFi names are temporary, low-stakes, and purely for entertainment. You can change them as often as you want. They are the digital equivalent of a lawn sign — a small expression of personality that neighbors and visitors will see.

Tips for Choosing a WiFi Name

  1. Keep it clean — Remember, neighbors and their kids can see it.
  2. Make it memorable — A funny name is easier to remember than "NETGEAR-5G."
  3. Change it seasonally — Holiday-themed names are always fun.
  4. Set a strong password — A funny name does not mean a weak password.
