Window Cleaning Business Name Ideas for Residential and Storefront Services

Need a window cleaning company name? Use these tips to create a bright, clean brand for residential and storefront window services.

Let the result show up in the name

Window cleaning is a visual service. Names that suggest shine, clarity, glass, brightness, or view quality often work because they make the outcome easy to picture for the customer.

Keep the tone clean and light

This category usually works best with names that feel fresh and tidy rather than heavy or technical. You want the name to feel as clean as the windows you promise to deliver.

Think about both homes and storefronts

If you may serve both residential and commercial clients, avoid a name that feels too narrow. A flexible name helps if you later add storefront routes or office work.

Check how it sounds in referrals

Window cleaning services often grow through local recommendations. A name that sounds natural when spoken out loud has a real advantage over one that only looks clever in writing.
