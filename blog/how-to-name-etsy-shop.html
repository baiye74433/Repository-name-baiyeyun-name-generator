How to Name Your Etsy Shop: A Step-by-Step Guide

Your Etsy shop name is your brand on one of the world's largest handmade marketplaces. Here is the complete naming process.

Step 1: Define Your Niche

Before naming your shop, get clear on what you sell. "Handmade goods" is too broad. "Personalized leather journals" is specific. The more specific your niche, the easier it is to name your shop.

Step 2: Brainshop 20+ Names

Write down every name that comes to mind. Do not judge them yet. Include descriptive names, creative names, personal names, and abstract names. The best name usually comes after the 15th or 20th idea.

Step 3: Apply the Etsy Search Test

Etsy's search algorithm considers shop names when ranking results. If your shop sells stickers and "Sticker" is in your name, you have a slight advantage for sticker-related searches. This does not mean you must include a keyword, but it helps for new shops without established sales history.

Step 4: Check Availability

  • Search Etsy for existing shops with the same name
  • Check if the .com domain is available
  • Verify Instagram and TikTok handles
  • Search Google for the name to check for conflicts

Step 5: Test with Real Buyers

Show your top 5 names to people who buy on Etsy. Ask them: "Would you click on a shop with this name?" Their reactions will tell you more than any naming guide.

Etsy Shop Name Rules

  • Must be 4-20 characters
  • Can only contain letters, numbers, and hyphens
  • Cannot contain spaces or special characters
  • Must be unique across all Etsy shops
  • Cannot be changed after your first sale (choose carefully!)
