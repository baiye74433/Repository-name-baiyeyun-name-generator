Etsy Shop Name Ideas: How to Stand Out on Etsy

Your Etsy shop name appears on every listing, every review, and every social media share. Here is how to choose one that sells.

Why Etsy Shop Names Are Special

Etsy is a marketplace where buyers browse by category, search by keyword, and judge shops by their name and first impression. Your shop name needs to work within Etsy's ecosystem while also being memorable enough for repeat customers.

Tips for Etsy Shop Naming

  1. Include your niche keyword — "Sticker" or "Jewelry" in the name helps with Etsy search.
  2. Keep it short — Long names get truncated in search results.
  3. Make it brandable — You might expand beyond Etsy someday.
  4. Check the .com domain — Even if you start on Etsy, having your own site later is valuable.

Common Etsy Naming Mistakes

  • Using your personal name — Unless you are already known, personal names do not attract new buyers.
  • Too generic — "Handmade Shop" does not stand out in search.
  • Copying successful shops — This creates confusion and can get you reported.
